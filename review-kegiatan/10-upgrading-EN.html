UPGRADING HIMATIKA 2024

Karanganyar, 25-26 Mei 2024

Hello Mathematicians!

It’s hard to believe that the 2024 HIMATIKA administration has been running for four months already. How do you feel, friends? Have you settled in over these four months? You should have by now. On Saturday and Sunday, May 25-26, HIMATIKA 2024 held one of the mandatory activities for the board members, namely Upgrading 2024, which took place at Rumah Revolusi Mental WCS. This activity aimed to build a sense of belonging among the board members towards HIMATIKA FMIPA UNS. The theme of this event was "Stepping Up Our Family and Progressivity Together for a Better HIMATIKA."

The event began with a presentation by Mas Afif Zakiy Abdullah, a former HIMATIKA board member. After the introductory presentation, the event continued with a Q&A session, where the board members were very enthusiastic in asking questions. The final session was an interactive discussion to solve a problem. In this session, the board members were divided into small forums, each with a specific topic to be presented in the main forum later. This interactive discussion was very conducive, structured, and of course, interactive.

The next event in the evening was group performances, opened by the event committee, followed by each group representative drawing lots for the performance order. There were two types of performances determined by the event committee: a flash mob and a drama. The following morning's activities included a morning exercise led by fitness instructors Mas Agrip and Mas Ihsan. After the exercise, there was an outbound activity guided by the DPO and former board members, consisting of three stations: Water Relay (Estafet Air), Water Tray Relay (Estafet Nampan Air), and Pingpong Ball Blow (Tiup Bola Pingpong). The groups were then re-divided to find the HIMATIKA puzzle pieces hidden by the DPO. The final event was the awarding of prizes to the winners.

That was the excitement of this year’s upgrading event. Hopefully, with this upgrading, all the HIMATIKA 2024 board members can feel the sense of family.
