Quality Time of HIMATIKA 2024

Surakarta, 9 Juni 2024

HIMATIKA FMIPA UNS organized the Quality Time 2024 event on June 9, 2024, at the FMIPA UNS Basketball Court. This event was attended by all members of the HIMATIKA 2024 board.

During the event, the board members were divided into eight groups tasked with completing a series of competitive games. The event was opened by the Bureau of Konsolidasi Lembaga, followed by a technical explanation of the activities to be carried out.

There were three types of games played: stacking cups into a pyramid shape, crab walk, and picture guessing. The competition began with the cup stacking game, played by four members from each group, followed by the crab walk, also played by four members, and concluded with the picture guessing game, where all group members worked together to answer the questions given. Cooperation was key for each group to quickly complete all three games.

The event was lively and filled with excitement, with all groups striving hard to complete the games. Despite not all groups winning, the board members appeared happy and enjoyed the competition.

After all the games were finished, the event continued with the announcement of the winners and the awarding of prizes. The event closed with a group photo session of all HIMATIKA board members. After the closing session, the board members took some time to chat and have fun together.

The Quality Time 2024 event not only strengthened the bonds among board members but also created beautiful memories that will be cherished by all HIMATIKA FMIPA UNS members. See you at the next Quality Time!
